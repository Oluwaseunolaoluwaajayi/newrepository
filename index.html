About Us

About Us Ojogbonn is an International Freight-Forwarding & Procurement company based in the Nigeria, with parthners all over the world.
We specialise in shipping (air & sea) between the UK & Nigeria, but our service covers origins worldwide coordinated through our parthners. Ojogbonn logistics has partners across the globe, our reach covering the continents of Africa, Europe, Asia, America & Australasia, all coordinated through a modern information technology network.
We are proficient in customs brokerage, supply chain, project management and logistics support. Ojogbonn logistics understands that global trade and industry markets are continuously changing and expanding. We are well positioned to meet and surpass customer needs and expectations through our procurement, logistics, air, sea/ocean freight forwarding, road haulage, distribution and warehousing solutions which are not only flexible but tailored to meet our clients' individual and ever-changing requirements.
