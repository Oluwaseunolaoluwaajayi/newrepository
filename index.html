About Us

About Us Ojogbonn is an International Freight-Forwarding & Procurement company based in the Nigeria, with parthners all over the world.
We specialise in shipping (air & sea) between the UK & Nigeria, but our service covers origins worldwide coordinated through our parthners. Ojogbonn logistics has partners across the globe, our reach covering the continents of Africa, Europe, Asia, America & Australasia, all coordinated through a modern information technology network.
We are proficient in customs brokerage, supply chain, project management and logistics support. Ojogbonn logistics understands that global trade and industry markets are continuously changing and expanding. We are well positioned to meet and surpass customer needs and expectations through our procurement, logistics, air, sea/ocean freight forwarding, road haulage, distribution and warehousing solutions which are not only flexible but tailored to meet our clients' individual and ever-changing requirements.

Our Mission

Our Mission Guided by our Commitment: Ojogbonn logistics venture is guided by our commitment to consistently deliver services which meet and exceed our customers’ expectations in all business transactions. Driven by Excellence: Ojogbonn logistics venture is driven to be the preferred choice for Procurement, Logistics Management, Shipping and Freight businesses by consistently understanding and exceeding our customers' expectations. Ojogbonn logistics provides first class and reliable total transportation solutions our services incorporate both Air and Ocean freight on a door-to-door basis, a direct link with Customs enables us to expedite the processing of customs documentation efficiently. Through our global network of partners, we provide a range of services from basic transportation to major logistics projects, all tailored to individual customer needs. Through our business-to-business offerings, we provide freight delivery services to small and large companies in the UK & Nigeria from all over the world. We also serve end users through value-for-money offerings. Our global operations span six continents: AFRICA: Nigeria, Cameroon, Kinshasa, Senegal, Angola and Kenya; EUROPE: Aberdeen, London, Norway, Italy, France, Germany; NORTH AMERICA: Houston, New York, Los Angeles, Dallas, Canada; SOUTH AMERICA: Rio De Janeiro; ASIA: UAE (Dubai), India, China; AUSTRALIA: Australia.
